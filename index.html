About Hawaii

Hawaii is a very beautiful place. There are many cool islands that are clos, that you can travel too. There is a lot of delicious food, such as poke, shaves ice, and meat. The different islands are:

  • Kauai
  • Oahu
  • Molokai
  • Lanai
  • Maui

The food in Hawaii

Hawaii has lots of different food. The most common food you can find is poke, which is marinated cut raw fish over rice. There is also musubi, usually with spam. You can find almost everything, but the most common dish food is fish. As everywhere is surrounded by water.
